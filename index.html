Dr. Ramin (Ron) Etemad obtained his Bachelor of Science in Biochemistry from Concordia University in 1993 and his D.D.S. degree (Doctor of Dental Surgery) from McGill University in 1997 in Montreal, Canada. He then moved to Ottawa and has been practicing in Kanata ever since. For the past 13 years he has completed hundreds of cosmetic smile enhancement cases, from bonding, porcelain veneers and implants to full mouth rehabilitation. His unique vision and creativity has not only served him in the field of cosmetic and reconstructive dentistry but also in everyday situations, from designing and making his own front desk at the office to making short films that were showcased at international film festivals. Dr. Etemad has a fleet of loyal patients that followed him as far away as Prescott, Ontario when he moved to his new practice ‘West Dental’ in 2009. In this new location he continues to offer his patients the same quality dental work that he’s been doing all these years; that’s the only way he knows. Dr. Etemad is married with two children. On the side, he enjoys painting, black & white photography and writing.
